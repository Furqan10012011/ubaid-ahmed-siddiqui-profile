# Ubaid Ahmed Siddiqui — Executive Personal Brand Website

## Overview

A premium executive personal brand website developed for **Mr. Ubaid Ahmed Siddiqui**, Senior HR & Administration Leader with over 15 years of experience in Human Resources, Talent Management, Workforce Development, Employee Relations, and Organizational Growth.

The website combines modern corporate aesthetics, executive storytelling, glassmorphism effects, responsive design, and professional branding to create a strong digital presence for recruiters, business leaders, HR professionals, and corporate stakeholders.
